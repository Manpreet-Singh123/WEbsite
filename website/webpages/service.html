Student Run Mess Committee

Student Run Mess Committee (SRMC)

As the Student-Run Mess Committee, our hearts are filled with gratitude and excitement as we reflect on our journey. What began as a vision to enhance the dining experience on campus has transformed into a beautiful reality that we proudly present to you. Every day, we embark on this culinary adventure with passion and determination. It is not just about serving meals; it is about creating a welcoming space where friendships are forged, laughter echoes, and memories are made.

Our success lies in the unity of our team. Together, we navigate challenges, celebrate diversity, and innovate with each menu. We owe our achievements to the unwavering support of the student body, whose enthusiasm fuels our drive to exceed expectations.As we turn each page of this collection, we hope our dedication to culinary excellence and community spirit shines through. Join us in savoring the taste of togetherness and the joy of shared experiences.
